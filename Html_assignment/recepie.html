Favourite Dish

My favourite food is Paneer Butter Masala.

Ingredients Required:

  • 450 gm paneer.
  • 5 medium chopped tomato.
  • 2 tablespoon powdered coriander seeds.
  • 1 1/2 tablespoon fresh cream.
  • 4 tablespoon butter.
  • 2 clove.
  • 2 red chilli.
  • 2 teaspoon garlic paste.

Steps Required To Cook:

  1. Heat oil and butter in a pan
  2. Add ginger-garlic paste and saute till the raw aroma disappears.
  3. Add the tomato puree and stir well
  4. Then add cashew paste and stir well
  5. Add water and stir
  6. Add julienned ginger and green chilies, salt and sugar and simmer till the curry begins to thicken
